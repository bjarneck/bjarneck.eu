Welcome to my website! 

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Political Science at the Université libre de Bruxelles (ULB), affiliated with the [CEVIPOL research centre](https://cevipol.phisoc.ulb.be/en/about). I defended my PhD in June 2026 and remain funded by the Walloon Fund for Scientific Research [(F.R.S.–FNRS)](https://www.frs-fnrs.be/en/) through October 2027. Previously, I studied at the University of Groningen (BA), the University of Maastricht (MSc), and the University of Cologne (MA).

In my [dissertation](/assets/Dissertation_ECK_online.pdf), I studied how election periods shape affective polarization and how affective polarization, in turn, influences electoral behaviour. My doctoral research was situated within the Belgian inter-university [NotLikeUs project](https://notlikeus.be) and funded by a four-year F.R.S.–FNRS Aspirant Fellowship. In Fall 2024, I was a visiting researcher at the [Canada Research Chair in Electoral Democracy](https://www.chairedemocratie.com) at the Department of Political Science at the Université de Montréal.

More broadly, my research focuses on political behaviour, elections, and European politics. I am also very interested in attitudes toward European integration and foreign policy.
